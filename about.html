About Janusz

Born in Poland and England based after living in Germany for 24 years. At the start of my career I worked as a carpenter before studying Art at Stuggart State Academy of Fine Arts in Germany and continuing to study at Gdansk Academy of Fine Arts in Poland. After I finished my studies, I worked at Wilhelma Theater at Stuggart State University of Music and Performing Arts as a stage technician for 7 years. I then moved onto the role of Workshop Manager at the Figure Theatre Department in Stuggart State University, where I taught visual design specialising in object and figure construction for a further 7 years.

More recently, during the past year and a half I have been working in traditional wooden boat building at Butler & Co Penpol in Cornwall. Since the beginning of my studies and in parallel to my employment I have been working as a freelance artist as well as collaborating with various theater performances & others artists.
