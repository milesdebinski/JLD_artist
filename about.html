About Janusz

Originally from Poland, and before I came to England I lived in Germany for 24 years. After I was employed as a stage technician at the Wilhelma Theater at the State University of Music and Performing Arts Stuttgart. I held this role for 7 years. I then moved into the role of Workshop Manager at the Figure Theatre Department of the State University of Music and Performing Arts Stuttgart, where I taught visual design - objects and figure construction. For a further 7 years. For the past year and a half I have been working in traditional wooden boat building Butlr & co company Penpol.
